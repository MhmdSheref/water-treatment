The Problem

Egypt's Growing Water Crisis

With per capita water availability plummeting from 2,000 m³/year in the 1960s to roughly 560 m³/year today, Egypt faces chronic water scarcity. Agriculture consumes 80% of freshwater while traditional irrigation loses 30–40% of water volume.

Methodology

How the DST Works

The tool integrates environmental engineering kinetics with multi-objective optimization to pinpoint bottlenecks and dynamically size hybrid treatment systems for Grade A compliance.

Σ

Water Quality Index

Aggregates multiple parameters into a single WQI score using AHP-weighted quality ratings against Egyptian Code ECP 501-2015.

Δ

Deviation Analysis

Computes the deviation vector D = P − S between plant readings and regulatory limits, with tolerance thresholds for measurement uncertainty.

λ

Reactor & UV Sizing

First-order decay kinetics for biological reactor sizing and Chick-Watson law for UV disinfection dosage — translated into actionable engineering specs.

⊕

MILP Optimization

Mixed-Integer Linear Programming balances CAPEX, OPEX, energy consumption, and nutrient recovery to find the optimal upgrade strategy.
